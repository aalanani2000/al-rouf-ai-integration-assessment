# Shipping and Delivery Terms

## Delivery Areas
AL ROUF LED Lighting Technology Co. ships throughout the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and to select GCC countries including UAE, Bahrain, and Kuwait. International shipments outside the GCC are handled on a case-by-case basis for bulk orders exceeding 500 units.

## Standard Delivery Timelines
- Riyadh metropolitan area: 2-3 business days
- Other major Saudi cities (Jeddah, Dammam, Khobar): 4-6 business days
- Remote areas within Saudi Arabia: 7-10 business days
- GCC international: 10-14 business days

## Bulk Order Lead Times
Orders exceeding 200 units of a single SKU may require additional production lead time of 5-10 business days before the standard delivery timeline begins, depending on current inventory levels. Customers will be notified of the exact lead time at the time of order confirmation.

## Shipping Costs
Shipping costs are calculated based on order weight, volume, and destination. Orders within Riyadh exceeding 10,000 SAR in value qualify for free delivery. A detailed shipping quote is provided alongside every formal quotation.

## Damage and Inspection
All shipments must be inspected at the time of delivery. Any visible damage to packaging must be noted on the delivery receipt and reported to AL ROUF within 48 hours to qualify for replacement under the shipping damage policy.
