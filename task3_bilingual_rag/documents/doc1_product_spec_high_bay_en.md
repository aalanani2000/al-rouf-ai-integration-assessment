# LED High Bay Light — 150W Technical Specification

## Product Overview
The AL ROUF 150W LED High Bay Light is designed for industrial and warehouse applications requiring high-output, energy-efficient illumination at mounting heights of 6 to 12 meters.

## Technical Specifications
- Power consumption: 150W
- Luminous flux: 21,000 lumens
- Luminous efficacy: 140 lm/W
- Color temperature: 5000K (neutral white), 4000K available on request
- Beam angle: 120 degrees
- Input voltage: 100-277V AC, 50/60Hz
- IP rating: IP65 (dust and water resistant)
- Operating temperature range: -30°C to +50°C
- Expected lifespan: 50,000 hours (L70)
- Dimming: 0-10V dimmable, DALI compatible on request

## Mounting Options
The fixture supports hook mount, U-bracket mount, and pendant mount configurations. Standard mounting hardware is included; bracket-specific hardware is available as an accessory.

## Certifications
CE, RoHS, SASO (Saudi Standards). The product complies with Saudi Electricity Regulatory Authority lighting efficiency requirements.

## Warranty
This product carries a 5-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use conditions. The warranty does not cover damage from improper installation, voltage outside the rated range, or unauthorized modification.
